Get to know us!

MANALI STRAYS is a registered non-profit animal welfare organization established on 3rd January, 2020 in Manali, Himachal Pradesh. Founded by Kamesh Singh Bora, our journey began in 2019 with the mission to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray and abandoned animals in the Manali region.

Our work goes beyond providing shelter—we ensure every rescued animal receives proper medical treatment, nutrition, vaccinations, sterilization, and care. We also run awareness campaigns to promote responsible pet adoption and humane treatment of animals.

Thanks to the generous support of donors, volunteers, and well-wishers, Manali Strays continues to create a safer, kinder environment for animals. Every contribution helps us provide food, medicine, and shelter to animals in need.

Our Vision

A future where no animal suffers on the streets of Manali—where every stray is given dignity, love, and a safe home.

Our Mission

  • Rescue and rehabilitate injured, sick, and abandoned animals.
  • Run sterilization and vaccination drives to control the stray population humanely.
  • Promote adoption of rescued animals into loving homes.
  • Raise awareness on compassion and responsible pet care.
